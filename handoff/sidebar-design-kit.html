Design Kit · Figma Handover · Dark

Maropost AppSidebar

Static reference for every sidebar state and submenu in the production dark color mode. All measurements, colors, and spacing match the live component (src/components/layout/AppSidebar.vue + src/styles/sidebar-dark.css). The prototype-only dark-sidebar toggle and accent color picker are excluded — this kit ships the dark sidebar itself, not the UI that toggles it.

Variant 02

Rail sidebar (64px)

Icon-only collapsed mode. Brand becomes the 32px "M" mark in a rounded square (white on dark). Hovering any icon opens its flyout card (see variants 6–13). Toggle pill anchored below the brand.

M
Width 64px. Brand box 32 × 32, 10px radius, white fill (token-text) on dark bg (token-bg). NEW badges and labels hidden — locked-state lock icon also hidden in rail.

Variant 03

Header & brand anatomy

Brand block in both modes, with the toggle pill positioned half-outside the right edge (centered vertically when expanded, dropped below brand in rail mode). Pill border at 22 % white, drop shadow deeper in dark mode to read against the navy.
